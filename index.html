Welcome To Suraburdi Meadows

SURABURDI MEADOWS – A perfect place to celebrate Weddings and Memorable Occasions, hold Conventions & Conferences and Special Events.

Ideally situated in a valley, surrounding a lake with wide spread lush green lawns – it is conveniently located at a distance of 14 kms. from Nagpur city centre… boasting of an arena of facilities.

It is quintessential choice to bring alive any occasion. In the bridal finery, this is the perfect setting to commemorate the historic events of your life.

Services

Suraburdi Meadows is ‘one stop shop’ for celebrating memorable moments and happy commemorations. We render a ‘360 degree’ solution to all your keen concerns and elusive imagination. We execute with personalized care all our Weddings / Events …conceptualizing and creating magnificent stages, elegant floral decorations, lighting effects, audio-visuals and delectable arrangement of food & beverages along with an option for you to unwind at our in-house luxurious accommodations.

It is not only a SPA but a complete Health & Wellness center. To ensure a peaceful encounter with oneself enjoy our foot reflexology and sprawling body massage.
